About Me

Getting to Know Me

Hello! I'm Marvel Zhang, a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Information Science with a certificate in Data Science. I'm passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact through data-driven insights.

My academic journey has been focused on understanding how information systems can be designed to better serve human needs. Through my coursework in Big Data Systems, Artificial Intelligence, and Human-Computer Interaction, I've developed a comprehensive understanding of both the technical and human aspects of information technology.

My Background

Originally from Sterling, Illinois, I'd been lost on what to do or what dreams to pursue, but recently I've been fascinated by the intersection of technology and human behavior. This interest led me to pursue Information Science, where I could explore how we can design better systems for storing, processing, and presenting information.

What Drives Me

I believe that data tells stories, and my goal is to become proficient at uncovering these stories to drive better decision-making. Whether it's analyzing market trends for product development or creating user-friendly interfaces for complex systems, I'm committed to making technology more accessible and useful for everyone.
